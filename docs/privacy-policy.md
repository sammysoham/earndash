# EarnDash Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

## 1. Overview

EarnDash collects and processes limited personal, device, and usage information to operate the app, attribute rewards, prevent fraud, secure accounts, review withdrawals, and comply with partner obligations. By using EarnDash, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

EarnDash may collect:

- account details such as email address, display name, referral code, and login method
- device details such as device fingerprint, app version, advertising identifier when available, and operating system data
- network and location-related data such as IP address, country detection, anti-VPN signals, and coarse geolocation indicators
- reward and activity data such as wallet balances, offer completions, ad reward claims, streak data, leaderboard preferences, withdrawal requests, and referral activity
- movement and fitness metadata such as step summaries, active minutes, device activity classifications, Health Connect summaries, and anti-cheat signals
- support, moderation, and audit information such as fraud reviews, admin notes, and audit log records

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- create and manage user accounts
- verify reward eligibility and maintain pending, available, and lifetime balances
- detect fraud, abuse, duplicate devices, suspicious networks, and prohibited VPN or proxy use
- process login streaks, gamification progress, referrals, and leaderboard placement
- review, approve, reject, or delay withdrawals
- troubleshoot app issues, improve performance, and analyze product usage
- communicate important service, security, and account notices

## 4. Health, Movement, and Sensor Data

If you use Move & Earn, EarnDash may process device motion and step-related information, including native sensor readings, Health Connect summaries, activity recognition labels, and anti-cheat validations. We use this information only to operate movement rewards, detect unrealistic activity, and improve tracking accuracy. Movement rewards may be reduced, held, or blocked if signals cannot be verified.

## 5. Ads, Offers, and Third-Party Partners

EarnDash works with advertising, analytics, authentication, and reward partners. These providers may receive identifiers or event data required to serve ads, verify rewarded views, measure offer completions, secure sign-in, or attribute activity. Partner policies may also apply when you interact with their services.

## 6. Fraud Prevention and Safety Controls

EarnDash uses automated and manual systems to assess account risk. This may include device matching, IP duplication checks, VPN and proxy detection, reward pattern analysis, referral abuse analysis, and withdrawal review. If our systems detect elevated risk, we may limit rewards, hold balances, anonymize leaderboard placement, disable withdrawals, or block the account.

## 7. Leaderboards and Public Visibility

If you opt in to leaderboard visibility, your chosen display name, rank, and earnings-related placement may appear inside the app. If you opt out, we may still show an anonymous or masked entry to preserve ranking integrity.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate EarnDash, reconcile rewards, investigate abuse, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain internal records. We may keep audit and fraud-review data for longer where necessary to protect the platform.

## 9. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary meaning of that term. We may share information with:

- service providers supporting hosting, authentication, analytics, notifications, and app operations
- reward, ad, payout, and offer partners when needed for verification and attribution
- legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity
- internal admin and moderation systems for fraud review, payout handling, and support resolution

## 10. Your Choices

Depending on product availability, you may be able to:

- update your display name
- choose whether your leaderboard name is public or anonymous
- stop using fitness-related features
- request account deletion or support review through EarnDash support channels

Some data may still be retained where needed for fraud prevention, auditability, payment records, or legal compliance.

## 11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account and reward data. However, no system is perfectly secure, and EarnDash cannot guarantee absolute security of transmitted or stored information.

## 12. Children's Privacy

EarnDash is not intended for users who are not legally allowed to participate in reward-based services in their jurisdiction. If we believe an account was created in violation of applicable age or eligibility rules, we may restrict or close it.

## 13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of EarnDash after an update means you accept the revised policy.

## 14. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact EarnDash support through the app or the support channel provided by the operator.
